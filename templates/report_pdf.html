Understanding Your Tier Classification

A-Tier (80-100 points)

Publisher-ready or near-ready. Strong concept with clear market positioning, established author platform, and professional execution. These proposals are competitive for traditional publishing with major houses.

B-Tier (60-79 points)

Promising with focused work needed. Good foundation but requires development in 1-2 key areas before submission to agents or publishers. With refinement, these proposals have strong potential.

C-Tier (40-59 points)

Significant development needed. Multiple areas require substantial work. May benefit from professional ghostwriting or developmental editing services to reach publishable quality.

D-Tier (0-39 points)

Not ready for traditional publishing in current form. Consider alternative publishing paths or substantial reimagining of the project. Professional development services recommended.

About Advance Estimates

Advance estimates are based on current market conditions for non-fiction books. Most first-time authors receive advances in the $0-$10,000 range. Higher advances typically require:

  • Established platform with significant reach (50,000+ followers/subscribers)
  • Proven track record (previous books, major media presence)
  • Timely topic with clear market demand
  • Strong competitive positioning
