📖 What is a book proposal?

A nonfiction book proposal is the document you send to literary agents and publishers before the book is written. It's typically 20–50 pages and answers one question: "Why will this book sell?"

Hook & Concept The one-sentence pitch that makes an editor lean forward
Target Reader Who will buy this book — and why they need it now
Comp Titles Similar books that prove the market exists
Author Bio Why YOU are the right person to write this book
Chapter Outline A map of the book, chapter by chapter
Sample Writing 1–3 chapters showing your voice and depth
Marketing & Platform Your reach and your plan to help sell the book
